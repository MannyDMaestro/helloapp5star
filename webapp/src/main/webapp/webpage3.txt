<span class="heading-toc" id="how-to-perform-fatalities-in-mortal-kombat-11"></span> <h2 id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-3" class="comp mntl-sc-block reference-sc-block-heading mntl-sc-block-heading"> <span class="mntl-sc-block-heading__text"> How to Perform Fatalities in Mortal Kombat 11 </span> </h2>
<p id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-4" class="comp mntl-sc-block mntl-sc-block-html">
Each of the 25 playable characters has two fatalities, which makes for a grand total of 50 unique finishing moves. Fatalities can only be performed at the end of the final round. When you see "Finish Him/Her" on the screen, stand in the appropriate position and input the correct button sequence to execute your opponent.
</p>
<div id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-5" class="comp mntl-sc-block mntl-sc-block-adslot mntl-block"></div>
<p id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-6" class="comp mntl-sc-block mntl-sc-block-html">
The button combinations for default fatalities can be found in each character's Move List. From the main menu, go to <strong>Learn</strong> &gt; <strong>Fatality Training</strong> to practice.
</p>

<span class="heading-toc" id="how-to-perform-fatal-blows"></span> <h2 id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-14" class="comp mntl-sc-block reference-sc-block-heading mntl-sc-block-heading"> <span class="mntl-sc-block-heading__text"> How to Perform Fatal Blows </span> </h2>
<p id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-15" class="comp mntl-sc-block mntl-sc-block-html">

</p>

<span class="heading-toc" id="how-to-give-mercy"></span> <h2 id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-17" class="comp mntl-sc-block reference-sc-block-heading mntl-sc-block-heading"> <span class="mntl-sc-block-heading__text"> How to Give Mercy </span> </h2>
<p id="mntl-sc-block_1-0-18" class="comp mntl-sc-block mntl-sc-block-html">

</p>
